Welcome to your dashboard!

As administrator you can now use extra pages in the menu on the upper right corner.
First of all, you can still take a look at the overview page, where you can see the ranking of the teams.

Now you can also add new team to the competition on the Add Team page.
To add a team, you will at least need a team name and you will have to choose a starting score.
In most cases the starting score will be 0, but if you want to start a team with another score that's possible as well.
If the team has a url to a team photo, you can add this link, but it's not required.

After each pubquiz you can update the scores for the teams on the Update Teams page.
On this page, the teams are sorted by the teams name for easy updating.
You can just fill in the points that where scored by each team.
In case of any mistakes, you can correct this by substracting points with the minus symbol.
If a team wants to drop out of the competition, you can also delete a team with the button on this page.

Lastly, you can use the contact page in case any problems with the site occur.
